    How to ues?

  1. Visit the website: Open your web browser and go to bannaifan.com.
  2. Select your preferred country: Choose the country you wish to view from the available options.
  3. Select the type of house: Choose the type of house you are interested in.
  4. Select the number of floors: Choose the number of floors for the property (Note: This option does not apply to condominiums).
  5. Click on the Submit button: Once you have made your selections, click the Submit button to view available properties.
  6. Select a property: If you find a property you are interested in, note down the property ID and click the Contact button.
  7. Complete the form: Fill in the required details in the contact form to inquire further about the property.
